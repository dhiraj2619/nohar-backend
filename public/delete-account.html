Delete Your Account

Last updated: 2 May 2026

This page explains how users of the Nohar app can request deletion of their account and personal data.

To request account deletion, email us from your registered email address or include your registered mobile number so we can verify the request.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to noharcosmetics@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Nohar Account".
  2. Include your registered mobile number and registered email address, if available.
  3. Our team will verify your request and process eligible account deletion within 7 to 30 days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile details
  • Your saved addresses
  • Your login or authentication details
  • Personal information linked directly to your app account

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained when required for legal, tax, accounting, fraud prevention, payment, delivery, dispute resolution, or regulatory purposes.

This may include order records, invoices, payment references, shipment information, or support history where retention is required.

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion, contact us at noharcosmetics@gmail.com.
